### Abstract:  
"Beyond and precisely because of the AI hype there is need for a systematic approach to engineer, develop, deploy and operate AI systems. If not applied along the whole lifecycle, there is no sustainable and commercial benefit of an AI system." 

*Dr. Thomas Usländer (Fraunhofer IOSB)*
### Bio 
Thomas Usländer holds a PhD in computer science from the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT). He is business developer for AI Systems Engineering in the Information Management and Production Control (ILT) department at Fraunhofer IOSB, Karlsruhe. His research focuses on AI Systems Engineering, industrial data spaces, open system architectures, and platform economics in Industrie 4.0. He is head of the subprojects Data Platform and AI Challenge of the AI Alliance Baden-Württemberg and head of the Karlsruhe Competence Center for AI Systems Engineering (CC-KING). He is a member of the Standardization Council Industrie 4.0 (SCI 4.0) and the DIN/DKE Expert Council AI. In 2017, Dr. Usländer received the DIN Innovation Award on Industrie 4.0.
LinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/in/tuslaender Research Gate: https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Thomas_Uslaender
