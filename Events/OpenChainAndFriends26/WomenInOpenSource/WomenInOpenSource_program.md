We  warmly invite you to join our Women in Open Source networking session at the OpenChain and Friends 2026 Event

📅 Women in Open Source Networking @ OpenChain and Friends Conference  
🕠 March 25, 2026 | 17:00–19:00  
📍 Robert Bosch GmbH – Service and Supply Chain Campus (SCC) Feuerbach, [Bregenzer Str. 26A, 70469 Stuttgart](https://www.google.com/maps/place//data=!4m2!3m1!1s0x4799dbd374e9c621:0xcad9aaa4488db0b?sa=X&ved=1t:8290&ictx=111)  
  
The session is designed as an open, moderated networking space for women and allies who work with, contribute to, or are simply curious about Open Source. Through themed discussion tables and guided conversations, we aim to create an inclusive environment for meaningful exchange: across technical, legal, business, and community perspectives.
  
During two open 30 minute discussion rounds, you’re free to choose the themed tables that spark your interest, connect with inspiring people around the Open Source topics that empower you, and move on after each round to explore fresh perspectives, learn from one another, and build meaningful connections that last well beyond our shared evening.
  
Themed tables can include (and are slightly oriented to the overall [event topic streams](https://openchainproject.org/news/2025/12/09/openchain-and-friends-2026#topicstreams) by intent):  
•	Communities & Events  
•	Compliance & OSPOs  
•	Artificial Intelligence & Cybersecurity  
•	Digital Sovereignty & Open Source in Business  
•	Automotive & Embedded and Open Hardware  
•	Education
